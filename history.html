Key Milestones in Human Space Activities

First Human in Space (1961)

Yuri Gagarin became the first human to travel into space aboard Vostok 1, marking a major milestone in space exploration.

Moon Landing (1969)

Apollo 11 successfully landed the first humans on the Moon, demonstrating the possibilities of human exploration beyond Earth.

International Space Station

The ISS represents global cooperation in space, serving as a research laboratory orbiting Earth.

Commercial Spaceflight

The rise of private companies like SpaceX and Blue Origin has revolutionized access to space, making it more affordable and frequent.
